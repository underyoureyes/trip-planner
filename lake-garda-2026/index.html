Stops & places of interest
🚲
MORNING — Road Bike Hire: Riva → Torbole → Nago loop
Book a road bike or e-bike at CCT Bike Rental (Arco, 3km from hotel — €8 taxi each way). Head north along the flat lakeside path from Riva to Torbole (~8km), then continue to Nago viewpoint if legs allow. Return the same route. Total ~28-32km, 2–3 hours riding, almost no elevation on the lakeside path. Riva → Torbole is one of the best easy flat rides on the lake. Start ~08:30-09:00 to be back for a late lunch. Book the bike online at least 2 days ahead.
📍 Map 🌐 Website
📷
AFTERNOON — Bastione Fortress walk
After the morning ride, a gentle afternoon option. Iconic 16th-century Venetian tower above the town — 30 min walk up through olive groves from Piazza III Novembre. Panoramic views over Riva and the lake. Easy well-signposted path. ~2,000 steps up and back. Free.
📍 Map
📷
AFTERNOON (alt) — Cascata del Varone waterfall
Dramatic gorge waterfall 3km from Riva centre — taxi ~€8 each way or 35 min walk. Two viewing platforms inside the gorge — genuinely spectacular. About 1,000 steps on site. A good shaded option on a hot afternoon.
📍 Map 🌐 Website
📷
AFTERNOON (alt) — Arco medieval town & castle
3km from Riva (€8 taxi). World-famous rock climbing destination. The ruined castle above the town is a short but steep walk (~1,500 steps return, 20 min). Lovely central piazza and coffee shops below for a post-walk espresso.
