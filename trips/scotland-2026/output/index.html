Duncryne Hill ('The Dumpling'), Gartocharn
Aim ~14:05. Hidden gem — a tiny round volcanic hill (142m) just off the A811 at Gartocharn giving what many regard as the finest panoramic view of Loch Lomond for the least effort anywhere: islands, Ben Lomond and the Highlands spread out below you. 35–40 min walk up and back, 88m ascent. Dogs welcome on open access land — on lead near livestock. Small layby parking on Duncryne Road (G83 8NE). Free.
